About Watrakan

A Strategic Partner For Investment & Development

Operating at the intersection of resources, capital and strategy. Watrakan combines legal, financial and commercial expertise to structure opportunities, build partnerships and create sustainable value across Suriname and the Caribbean.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable solutions for the Oil and Energy sector in Suriname and the Caribbean, empowering companies to achieve their full potential through innovative and reliable services. We aspire to create a future where our clients can seamlessly navigate the complexities of the industry, leveraging our expertise in risk analysis, information dissemination, and implementation strategies. By fostering a culture of fairness, feasibility, and sustainability, we aim to drive transformative growth and long-term success for our partners and the communities they serve.

Our Mission

To support clients in optimizing their financial performance and strategic decisions through expert advice and innovative solutions, as well as performing all activities related to the above in the broadest sense or that may be conducive to it. Furthermore, the Company strives to add value through accurate accounting services, in-depth financial analyses, and customized consultancy solutions that meet the unique needs of each client.
